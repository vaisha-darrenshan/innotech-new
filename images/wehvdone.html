Codescape

CODESCAPE challenges students to code a school app (live location!) or student portfolio platform. Winners get prizes, recognition, and potentially collab with Inno-tech council!

Mirai Estrellas

Mirai Estrellas is an innovation club organized by the innotech council to form an elite team of like-minded students who are interested in the field of STEAM. The name ‘Mirai Estrellas’ translates to future stars. This team includes students from Grade 5 to Grade 12. The aim of this group is to make a team that would be capable of participating in various competitions.

Tech Titans

Code It program which is named Tech Titans for just the Scouts. For this program, only the academic and innotech council will be working together as it is a skill-based subject on Python for the scouts.

Report Card Designing

The InnoTech council offered design expertise for the new report cards for Kindergarten and grades 1 & 2. This ensures a fresh look and potentially improved functionality for these reports.

PPT Designing for School Events

Inno-Tech council members designed captivating presentations for school events like the annual prize day. This elevated the visual impact and enhanced the overall experience.

GIC

The Global Innovation Competition focused on SDG goals, engaging numerous schools in an innovation contest. It was enriching to witness diverse projects from various schools, engage in conversations with judges, and receive valuable feedback.

SUSTAINX

SUSTAINX is another innovative event focused on sustainable development goals, encouraging students to create impactful projects that can contribute to a better future.
